United States of Secrets: Edward Snowden Part 1

  • Dec 2012: Glenn Greenwald – lead reporter.
  • Edward Snowden – leak “The Program”.
  • The Program begin in September 11, 2001.
  • NSA created to prevent surprise attack after WW2.
  • After 9/11 NSA start question themselves what they should be investigating to prevent these attacks.
  • Government begin monitoring foreign internet traffic coming into the United States.
  • But some agency wanted more, they want a whole new surveillance program without warrants, tracking all sort of information.
  • The problem: How can who detect these terrorist attack beforehand.
  • The solution: Monitoring every single person because we don’t know who is enemy and who is foe.
  • Head of NSA Hayden present the president an outline of “The program”: Used to gather data on phone calls and internet traffic of every single American to search for any suspicious connection that may deal with 9/11 or potential future threat.
  • The issues with “The program” is that part of the program is illegal.
  • The president authorize Hayden to start the program and on October 4, under a secret signing the program is officially in effect.
  • Thin Thread- a program that can eavesdrop on the entire world but with an encryption that protect the privacy of US citizens.
  • Government basically say no to the encryption, but yes to the eavesdropping.
  • The Program (Stellarwind): eavesdropping without a warrant.
  • The Program was finally brought to light on 2005, when New York Time publish the paper.
  • 2008 Obama decide to keep “The Program” because he is convinced that it was effective to stop terrorist attack.
  • Edward Snow expose The Program to the Press, with huge amount of evidence.
