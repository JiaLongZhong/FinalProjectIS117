Shoshana Zuboff on Surveillance Capitalism:

  • Misconception: People believe that everything online is control by themselves.
  • They believe that all information are willing given to the companies.
  • Since the person is the one who posted online.
  • But what the companies really get is your habit, your connection, and what you prefer. Basically, getting data about you to help predict your next movement.
  • These data can be used to lure you to buy something.
  • Companies said that they need data to provide better serves, which is true.
  • But the data is also used to create a model of you.
  • Meaning it study and analysis your human behavior.
  • That data is later put in a group of similar behavior people and use to predict your group future behavior. Also known as a behavioral surplus.
  • The dangerous part of this algorithm of data is the future prediction.
  • For instance, a lady just got pregnant and was waiting to tell her family at dinner, but her family start receive a span of baby products ads beforehand.
  • These data are so advance it knows the person better than the person themselves.
  • The data can be used to predict our personality, our emotion, how we view things, and how see things in a political view.
  • If you post a picture of yourself on Facebook.
  • Facebook is not interest in the events, but it is interest in your facial feature because it can be used to study your mood. For instance, it can now tell if you like something online, that you don’t want to admit it.
  • Data are being sold to business customer to maximum our value to their business.
  • Pokémon Go is a friendly kid game. -True.
  • Pokémon Go lure kids to store and business to gain profit. -True
  • The truth about this friendly game, is that there is another game being play by the business behind the scene.
