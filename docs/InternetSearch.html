Internet Search:

The second part of "The True Story of the Internet" is about search engines. Search engines play a very important role in our life today because they help us find the results we want when searching the web. This part of the series called Internet Search is mainly focused on how the uprising of search engines happened. The movie starts off with the narrator talking about how the world was a lot different before the invention of the search engine. The movie goes into Silicon Valley and talks about how Yahoo had started:

  • Silicon Valley is an area of California where many big tech companies are born out of.
  • Jerry Yang and David Filo were trying to use the internet to find a way to win a Stanford Fantasy Basketball league.
  • David Filo and Jerry Yang were electrical engineering students at Stanford.
  • David had figured out a way to search the web efficiently.
  • David and Jerry then created a big database of links with categories which allowed you to surf the web quickly although it was nothing like Yahoo today.
  • This was called David and Jerry Guide to the World Wide Web.

Eventually it had been renamed to Yahoo however they needed funding because it costs money to scale a project like this. It was very hard to find funding because at the time it was hard to make money on the internet. However some venture capitalists had believed the internet had huge potential for advertising although David and Jerry didn't believe in advertising. They were one of the people on the other side of the camp that believed the internet was for freedom and not advertising. After awhile search engine companies became very hungry and didn't focus on their product as much.This is when google had emerged. Google was coming out of Stanford as well. Google:

  • Work by link counting:The links that were visited the most were at the top of the search.
  • Excited(another company) was thinking about buying out google but they decided to decline buying them from $1,000,000.
  • Google did not want to put big banner ads on their website so they found another way to monetize.
  • They had sold keywords to companies which had allowed companies to change words in their site so their websites showed up at the top on Google.
  • This had brought lots of investors into Google and lots of interest.
